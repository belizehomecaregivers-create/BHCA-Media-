BELTRAIDE & CENPROMYPE — Belize Emprende Fund
2016
🏆 National Award
BHCA Wins Belize Emprende Fund — Supported by Republic of China (Taiwan)

BHCA was selected as the winning venture of the Belize Emprende Fund, awarded by BELTRAIDE and CENPROMYPE with the support of the Republic of China (Taiwan). Sasha discharged herself from hospital while suffering from pneumonia to present and receive the award — attending the ceremony alone, heplock still in her arm. She was rushed back to hospital immediately afterward.

"A poor 22 year old woman with no status from the ghetto trying to create an entire industry... I had pneumonia. I remember entering the room with my heplock still in arm. Drugged up, coughing, lungs burning — I still pushed through." — Sasha Eiley, December 2019
Source: Sasha N Eiley / Facebook

BHCA — NCA & PAHO/WHO Partnership
February 2021
🤝 Partnership
Mandatory COVID-19 Training for BHCA Caregivers — NCA & PAHO/WHO

BHCA partnered with the National Council on Ageing (NCA) and PAHO/WHO to make COVID-19 Infection Protection training mandatory for all registered BHCA caregivers. The free virtual training ran February 16–17, 2021 and demonstrated BHCA's formal standing as a recognized caregiver organization working alongside international health institutions.

"Please note this is a mandatory training session for registered BHCA Caregivers." — BHCA Facebook, February 2021
Source: BHCA Facebook Page
Breaking Belize News
June 16, 2021
📰 News Coverage
BHCA Secures Groceries for the Elderly Through Private Sector Partnership

Breaking Belize News covered BHCA's partnership with Grocery Nest — a diaspora-funded grocery programme for elderly Belizeans hit hardest by COVID-19. Launched with 10–15 sponsored recipients selected through churches, it allowed overseas Belizeans to purchase and deliver groceries digitally. Developed in collaboration with the NCA and a nutritionist.

"You have situations where everyone shows up on granny's birthday because she turned 90, and they're loving and take pictures — and granny doesn't see them again until she's 95." — Sasha Eiley, June 2021

7 News Belize — Tropical Vision Limited
2021
📺 TV Interview
Elder Abuse, Dementia & the Case for Regulating Caregiving in Belize

7 News Belize interviewed Sasha on the link between unregulated caregiving and elder abuse, highlighting that Alzheimer's and dementia patients are most vulnerable. She called for formal industry regulation to protect Belize's most at-risk seniors.

"Whenever there is a case about elder abuse, 99% it is Alzheimer and dementia patients... I find that dementia and Alzheimer patients are more vulnerable and they are high on the list of being abused." — Sasha Eiley, 7 News Belize, 2021

Greater Belize Media — Channel 5 / News 5
July 5, 2025
📰 News Coverage
Systemic Elder Abuse Alleged — BHCA Advocates for Mama Tona

Greater Belize Media covered BHCA's campaign for "Mama Tona," an 84-year-old who suffered a stroke after being repeatedly required to travel to Belize City to defend her land in court. The family spent over $12,000 complying with court demands. BHCA's advocacy prompted the NCA (via Justice Moore) to investigate.

"This is not just a land dispute; it is elder abuse. This is bullying." — BHCA Statement, July 2025

Key Advocacy Themes

Industry Regulation
National petition (503+ signatures), TV interviews, and public calls for mandatory screening and government oversight of caregiving in Belize.
Elder Abuse Prevention
Mama Tona campaign; dementia patient vulnerability advocacy; pushed NCA and judicial intervention; raised national awareness on elder rights.
Economic Support for Elderly
Soap Drive (2020) and Grocery Nest diaspora programme (2021) — two community-led initiatives responding directly to elderly poverty in Belize.
Government & Institutional Engagement
Presented at National Conference on Ageing 2024; PAHO/WHO & NCA training partnerships; Belize Emprende Fund winner; Red Cross collaboration.
Caregiver Professionalization
Founded 2010; launched publicly 2017; grew to 100+ certified caregivers and the only Special Needs Nanny provider in Belize; currently developing wound care and CNA training programmes to set professional standards for Belize's entire caregiving industry.
